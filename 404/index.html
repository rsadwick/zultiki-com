About

Discover the mysterious Isle of Zul'Tiki with classes such as Shaman, Bonder, Blood Drinker, Enchanter and Paladin. Use your healing, tanking, and utility prowess to outlast and defeat your enemies.

Live off the land and craft powerful armor and weapons that will guide you through your journey on the isle.

Defend the isle against powerful and destructive enemies that will stop at nothing to colonize and destroy.
